How it works
Park in 4 simple steps
From finding a spot to getting your receipt — the entire process takes just minutes.
1
Find Location
Browse the map or search for nearby malls, hospitals, hotels or parks in your area.
2
Pick a Slot
Choose any available green slot at your selected location. See floor-wise availability.
3
Book & Pay
Confirm your vehicle details and pay securely via UPI, card or cash at the counter.
4
Get Receipt
Receive a full digital receipt with booking ID, duration, and total fee paid.
